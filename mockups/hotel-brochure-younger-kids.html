Image Analysis for Younger Kids (3-6)

Best images for hotel parents:
• Image 1, 8, 20: Show happy, engaged younger children in safe environments
• Clear activities: Parents can see exactly what kids will be doing
• Professional setting: Shows structured care, not just play
• Mixed indoor/outdoor: Demonstrates variety and weather flexibility

Key visual elements that work:
• Younger children clearly visible (3-6 age range)
• Adult supervision visible or implied
• Clean, safe environments
• Active engagement (not passive sitting)
• Natural lighting showing real moments
