DL Size Notes

You were absolutely right! I was using A5 proportions (210x297) instead of DL (99x210).

DL is much narrower:
• 99mm wide x 210mm tall (1:2.12 ratio)
• Designed to fit in standard #10 envelopes
• Perfect for hotel brochure racks

Design adjustments needed:
• Smaller text sizes
• Tighter layouts
• Portrait images work better
• Less horizontal space for content
