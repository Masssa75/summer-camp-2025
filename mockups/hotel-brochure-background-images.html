Background Images Analysis

Pros of using background/nature images:
• More eye-catching: Bright, beautiful scenery grabs attention in hotel racks
• Aspirational: Shows the amazing Thai environment kids will explore
• Less "institutional": Feels more like vacation than daycare
• Emphasizes location: Highlights unique Phuket experiences

Cons to consider:
• No kids visible: Parents can't see age-appropriate activities
• Less trust-building: Scenery doesn't show safety/supervision
• More generic: Could be any tour company

Recommendation: Use for initial attention-grabbing, but include kids' photos inside the brochure.
