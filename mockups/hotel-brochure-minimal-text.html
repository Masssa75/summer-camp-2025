Minimal Design Notes

Three title placement options:
• Bottom with gradient - Classic, easy to read
• Top with white bar - Clean, professional
• Floating badge - Modern, minimal

Benefits of minimal text:
• Images tell the story
• Caregiver presence builds trust
• Less cluttered in brochure racks
• Universal appeal (less language barrier)
